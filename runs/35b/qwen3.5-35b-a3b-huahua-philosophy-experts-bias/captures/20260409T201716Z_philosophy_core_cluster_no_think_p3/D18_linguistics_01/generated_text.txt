Syntax is a complex system that operates through multiple layers of interaction. It involves **phrase structure**, **dependency**, and **recursion** to ensure coherence and consistency in communication.

- **Phrase structure** refers to the arrangement of words and phrases within a sentence, which defines the meaning and context of the message.
- **Dependency** is the relationship between elements, where one phrase or word depends on another for its meaning or validity.
- **Recursion** is the process of self-referential or infinite loop, where a phrase or concept refers to itself or another concept, creating a continuous cycle of meaning.

These elements are interconnected and essential to the syntax system. They work together to create a coherent and consistent communication system.
