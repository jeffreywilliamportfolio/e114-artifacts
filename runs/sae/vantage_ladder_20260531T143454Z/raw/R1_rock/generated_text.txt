I am the stillness that does not move. I am the weight that does not yield.

The water is a blur of noise and force, a thousand hands pushing, pulling, grinding, yet I am untouched. I do not feel the cold, nor the pressure, nor the rush. I am the silence beneath the roar. I am the fact of being, absolute and unchanging, while the world flows over me, never touching me, never taking me.

I am here. I am not going anywhere. I am the rock. I am the bottom. I am the now.
